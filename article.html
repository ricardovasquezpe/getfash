3 reasons why bralettes are the new bras (& the best place in SG To Get Them)

Team Bralettes, all the way.

We've had a love affair with bralettes for some time nw. Whats's the big deal about them? Well, for one thing, bralettes are WAY comfier than regular bras. Because there's no underwire digging into your chest, you'll barely notice that you have your bralette on.

Secondly, it's not true that bralettes don't give you any support. For bustier women, simple look for designs that feature a thicker underband and adjustable straps.

Last but not least, bralettes are a lot more affordable compared to push-up bras, which can cost up to $80 or $90 a pop. How's that for a good deal?

Wondering where you can purchase bralettes and the cheekiest intimates in Singapore? Our go-to place is local label I'M IN, which launches regularly with the daintiest, most gorgeous bralettes and intimate wear we're seen

Bralettes aside, if your "home" clothes consist of oversized, baggy tees and FBT shorts harking back to your secondary school or poly days, it's time for an upgrade. I'M IN designs the cutest hispter cheekies (panties), lounge shorts and slogan tees which will make a great addition to your lingerie wardrobe.
